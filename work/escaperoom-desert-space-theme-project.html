EscapeRoom: Desert & Space Theme Project

Read time:

6 min

Client:

Unreal

Industry:

Gaming & Entertainment

Start:

September 2, 2025

End:

December 18, 2025

Duration:

15 Weeks

This Unreal Engine 5 puzzle adventure project focuses on creating an immersive, multi-themed escape room experience. Players are challenged to solve complex environmental riddles and navigate through two distinct, highly detailed worlds—a vast desert ancient temple and a futuristic outer space station—built to test their intuition and problem-solving skills.

Starting point

The initial concept began with basic line-trace collision logic, but lacked environmental immersion and a progressive flow. The level structures were fragmented, making it simple to bypass mechanics without engaging with the intended theme or atmosphere. It also lacked a cohesive visual narrative to seamlessly transition the player from one distinct environment to the next.

Problem solving

My goal was to preserve a challenging puzzle mechanic while introducing high-end visual storytelling and rigid logical progression. I implemented a modular blueprint system that requires precise ray-casting data to detect interactive clues. I reorganized the level architecture, moving from a standard room layout to an escalating dual-world journey without compromising technical performance.

Implimentation

I built the interactive elements from scratch using Unreal Engine 5's advanced visual scripting Blueprints. The level design heavily utilizes dynamic lighting and atmospheric particle effects to enhance the contrast between the scorching, organic feel of the desert level and the cold, metallic isolation of the space environment. Responsive logic triggers were integrated to ensure that environmental props and puzzles animate seamlessly when approached or activated by the player.

Results

After finalizing the mechanical loop, the overall gameplay experience felt incredibly polished and tactile. The interaction feels sharp, the environmental transitions are striking, and the puzzle progression feels highly rewarding. This project gave my portfolio a robust showcase of blueprint logic and level design that feels as refined and thoughtful as a standalone puzzle experience.
